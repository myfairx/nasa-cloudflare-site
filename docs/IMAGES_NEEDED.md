## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Photo Quality

- **Good lighting:** Natural light or well-lit office
- **Focus:** Sharp and clear, not blurry
- **Composition:** Professional framing
- **Background:** Clean and uncluttered

### Smartphone Photography

- Clean your camera lens first
- Use natural light (near window or outside)
- Hold phone steady (use both hands)
- Tap screen to focus before taking photo
- Take multiple shots and pick the best

### Logo Guidelines

- **Vector format (SVG):** Best quality, scales to any size
- **PNG with transparency:** Good for web, needs transparent background
- **Avoid:** JPG logos (will have white background box)
- **Minimum size:** Logo should be clear at 200px wide
